CHHATTISGARH STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
===============================================
State-specific legal reference for Chhattisgarh laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN CHHATTISGARH
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN CHHATTISGARH
(a) Stamp duty rate: 5% of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 4% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Chhattisgarh — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Chhattisgarh through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-CH-... for Chhattisgarh e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN CHHATTISGARH
(a) Registration portal: bhuiyan.cg.nic.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN CHHATTISGARH
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN CHHATTISGARH
Applicable law: CG Urban Areas Rent Control Act
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN CHHATTISGARH
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN CHHATTISGARH
RERA portal: rera.cgstate.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN CHHATTISGARH
(a) Professional tax in CG: Up to Rs. 2,400 per year.
(b) CSIDCO: CG State Industrial Development Corporation.
(c) CG Shops and Establishments Act.
(d) Tribal land restrictions similar to Jharkhand in certain areas.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR CHHATTISGARH
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR CHHATTISGARH
(a) Always verify title documents in Chhattisgarh revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Chhattisgarh RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Chhattisgarh property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Chhattisgarh.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Chhattisgarh Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Chhattisgarh police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Chhattisgarh.
