JHARKHAND STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
============================================
State-specific legal reference for Jharkhand laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN JHARKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN JHARKHAND
(a) Stamp duty rate: 4% of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 3% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Jharkhand — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Jharkhand through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-JH-... for Jharkhand e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN JHARKHAND
(a) Registration portal: jharbhoomi.nic.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN JHARKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN JHARKHAND
Applicable law: Jharkhand Urban Areas Rent Control Act
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN JHARKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN JHARKHAND
RERA portal: jharkhandrera.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 4: STATE-SPECIFIC LAWS AND REGULATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN JHARKHAND
(a) CNT Act (Chotanagpur Tenancy Act 1908): Protects tribal land — significant restriction on sale of tribal land.
(b) SPT Act (Santhal Parganas Tenancy Act 1949): Restricts transfer of land in Santhal Parganas.
(c) Professional tax in Jharkhand: Up to Rs. 2,400 per year.
(d) JPDA: Jharkhand Planning and Development Authority.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR JHARKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR JHARKHAND
(a) Always verify title documents in Jharkhand revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Jharkhand RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Jharkhand property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Jharkhand.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Jharkhand Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Jharkhand police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Jharkhand.
