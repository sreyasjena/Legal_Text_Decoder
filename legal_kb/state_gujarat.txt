GUJARAT STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
==========================================
State-specific legal reference for Gujarat laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN GUJARAT
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN GUJARAT
(a) Stamp duty rate: 4.9% of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 1% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Gujarat — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Gujarat through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-GU-... for Gujarat e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN GUJARAT
(a) Registration portal: anyror.gujarat.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN GUJARAT
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN GUJARAT
Applicable law: Gujarat Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN GUJARAT
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN GUJARAT
RERA portal: gujrera.gujarat.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 4: STATE-SPECIFIC LAWS AND REGULATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN GUJARAT
(a) GIFT City: Special financial zone with separate regulations.
(b) AMC (Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation): Property tax and licenses.
(c) Professional tax in Gujarat: Up to Rs. 2,400 per year.
(d) Gujarat Shops and Establishments Act 1948.
(e) DMIC (Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor) passes through Gujarat.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR GUJARAT
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR GUJARAT
(a) Always verify title documents in Gujarat revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Gujarat RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Gujarat property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Gujarat.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Gujarat Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Gujarat police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Gujarat.
