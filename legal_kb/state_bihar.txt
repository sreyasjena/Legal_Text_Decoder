BIHAR STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
========================================
State-specific legal reference for Bihar laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN BIHAR
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN BIHAR
(a) Stamp duty rate: 6% for men, 5.7% for women of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 2% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Bihar — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Bihar through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-BI-... for Bihar e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN BIHAR
(a) Registration portal: biharbhumi.bihar.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN BIHAR
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN BIHAR
Applicable law: Bihar Rent Control Act applicable in certain areas
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN BIHAR
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN BIHAR
RERA portal: rerabihar.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR BIHAR
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR BIHAR
(a) Always verify title documents in Bihar revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Bihar RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Bihar property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Bihar.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Bihar Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Bihar police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Bihar.
