MADHYA PRADESH STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
=================================================
State-specific legal reference for Madhya Pradesh laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN MADHYA PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN MADHYA PRADESH
(a) Stamp duty rate: 7.5% (one of highest in India) of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 3% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Madhya Pradesh — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Madhya Pradesh through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-MA-... for Madhya Pradesh e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN MADHYA PRADESH
(a) Registration portal: mpbhulekh.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN MADHYA PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN MADHYA PRADESH
Applicable law: MP Accommodation Control Act 1961
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN MADHYA PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN MADHYA PRADESH
RERA portal: rera.mp.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR MADHYA PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR MADHYA PRADESH
(a) Always verify title documents in Madhya Pradesh revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Madhya Pradesh RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Madhya Pradesh property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Madhya Pradesh.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Madhya Pradesh Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Madhya Pradesh police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Madhya Pradesh.
