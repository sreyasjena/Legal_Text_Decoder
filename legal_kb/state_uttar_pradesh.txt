UTTAR PRADESH STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
================================================
State-specific legal reference for Uttar Pradesh laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN UTTAR PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN UTTAR PRADESH
(a) Stamp duty rate: 7% for men, 6% for women of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 1% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Uttar Pradesh — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Uttar Pradesh through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-UT-... for Uttar Pradesh e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN UTTAR PRADESH
(a) Registration portal: upbhulekh.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN UTTAR PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN UTTAR PRADESH
Applicable law: UP Rent Control Act 1972
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN UTTAR PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN UTTAR PRADESH
RERA portal: up-rera.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 4: STATE-SPECIFIC LAWS AND REGULATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN UTTAR PRADESH
(a) Professional tax in UP: Not applicable.
(b) LDA, Noida Authority, Greater Noida Authority: Development authorities.
(c) Bhulekh portal: UP online land records.
(d) UP Shops and Commercial Establishments Act 1962.
(e) Strong RERA enforcement in NCR region (Noida/Greater Noida).

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR UTTAR PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR UTTAR PRADESH
(a) Always verify title documents in Uttar Pradesh revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Uttar Pradesh RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Uttar Pradesh property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Uttar Pradesh.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Uttar Pradesh Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Uttar Pradesh police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Uttar Pradesh.
