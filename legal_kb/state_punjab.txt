PUNJAB STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
=========================================
State-specific legal reference for Punjab laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN PUNJAB
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN PUNJAB
(a) Stamp duty rate: 7% for men, 5% for women of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 1% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Punjab — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Punjab through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-PU-... for Punjab e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN PUNJAB
(a) Registration portal: plrs.org.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN PUNJAB
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN PUNJAB
Applicable law: Punjab Rent Act 1995
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN PUNJAB
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN PUNJAB
RERA portal: haryanarera.gov.in (shared with Haryana)
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN PUNJAB
(a) Professional tax in Punjab: Up to Rs. 2,400 per year.
(b) PUDA (Punjab Urban Development Authority).
(c) GMADA: Greater Mohali Area Development Authority.
(d) Punjab Shops and Commercial Establishments Act 1958.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR PUNJAB
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR PUNJAB
(a) Always verify title documents in Punjab revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Punjab RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Punjab property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Punjab.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Punjab Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Punjab police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Punjab.
