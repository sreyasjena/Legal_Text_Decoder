ODISHA STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
=========================================
State-specific legal reference for Odisha laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN ODISHA
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN ODISHA
(a) Stamp duty rate: 5% for men, 4% for women of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 2% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Odisha — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Odisha through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-OD-... for Odisha e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN ODISHA
(a) Registration portal: bhulekh.ori.nic.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN ODISHA
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN ODISHA
Applicable law: Odisha House Rent Control Act 1967
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN ODISHA
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN ODISHA
RERA portal: odisharerait.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR ODISHA
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR ODISHA
(a) Always verify title documents in Odisha revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Odisha RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Odisha property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Odisha.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Odisha Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Odisha police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Odisha.
