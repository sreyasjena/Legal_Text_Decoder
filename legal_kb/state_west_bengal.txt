WEST BENGAL STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
==============================================
State-specific legal reference for West Bengal laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN WEST BENGAL
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN WEST BENGAL
(a) Stamp duty rate: 7% (6% for women) of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 1% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in West Bengal — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for West Bengal through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-WE-... for West Bengal e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN WEST BENGAL
(a) Registration portal: banglarbhumi.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN WEST BENGAL
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN WEST BENGAL
Applicable law: West Bengal Premises Tenancy Act 1997
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN WEST BENGAL
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN WEST BENGAL
RERA portal: hira.wb.gov.in (HIRA — WB equivalent of RERA)
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 4: STATE-SPECIFIC LAWS AND REGULATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN WEST BENGAL
(a) Professional tax in West Bengal: Up to Rs. 2,400 — high compliance state.
(b) KMC (Kolkata Municipal Corporation): Property tax and trade licenses.
(c) HIDCO: New Town Kolkata development.
(d) WB Shops and Establishments Act 1963.
(e) Probate required for wills in West Bengal for immovable property.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR WEST BENGAL
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR WEST BENGAL
(a) Always verify title documents in West Bengal revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on West Bengal RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with West Bengal property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to West Bengal.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from West Bengal Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by West Bengal police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in West Bengal.
