HIMACHAL PRADESH STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
===================================================
State-specific legal reference for Himachal Pradesh laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
(a) Stamp duty rate: 5% for men, 4% for women of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 2% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Himachal Pradesh — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Himachal Pradesh through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-HI-... for Himachal Pradesh e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
(a) Registration portal: himbhoomi.hp.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
Applicable law: HP Urban Rent Control Act 1987
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
RERA portal: himrerait.hp.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 4: STATE-SPECIFIC LAWS AND REGULATIONS

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN HIMACHAL PRADESH
(a) Non-Himachali cannot purchase agricultural land in HP — significant restriction.
(b) Professional tax in HP: Up to Rs. 2,400 per year.
(c) HP Shops and Commercial Establishments Act 1969.
(d) Town and Country Planning Act HP: Development regulations.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR HIMACHAL PRADESH
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR HIMACHAL PRADESH
(a) Always verify title documents in Himachal Pradesh revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Himachal Pradesh RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Himachal Pradesh property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Himachal Pradesh.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Himachal Pradesh Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Himachal Pradesh police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Himachal Pradesh.
