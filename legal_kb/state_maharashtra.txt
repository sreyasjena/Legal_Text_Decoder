MAHARASHTRA STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
===============================================

1. MAHARASHTRA RENT CONTROL ACT, 1999
Applies to residential and commercial premises in Maharashtra. Key provisions:
(a) Leave and license agreements: Must be registered regardless of duration (Section 55).
(b) Standard rent: Fixed by Rent Court.
(c) Eviction grounds: Non-payment, subletting, nuisance, bona fide personal requirement.
(d) Pagdi system: Old tenancy system where tenant pays premium (pagdi) for occupation rights.
(e) 1% registration: All leave and license agreements must be registered with Sub-Registrar.

2. MAHARASHTRA STAMP ACT, 1958
(a) Sale deed: 5% stamp duty + 1% local body tax + 1% registration = approximately 7%.
(b) Gift deed: 3% for blood relatives, 5% for others.
(c) Leave and license agreement: Based on monthly rent and deposit amount formula.
(d) Mortgage: 0.5% on loan amount.
(e) e-Registration: Available in Maharashtra for leave and license agreements.

3. MAHARASHTRA APARTMENT OWNERSHIP ACT, 1970
(a) Governs apartment ownership and cooperative housing societies in Maharashtra.
(b) Cooperative housing societies: Governed by Maharashtra Cooperative Societies Act, 1960.
(c) Society NOC: Required for sale and rental of apartments.
(d) Share certificate: Evidence of membership in housing society.
(e) Transfer fee: Society charges transfer fee on sale of flat — capped by government.

4. MHADA (MAHARASHTRA HOUSING AND AREA DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY)
(a) Constructs and allots affordable housing in Maharashtra.
(b) Lottery system for allotment of MHADA flats.
(c) Resale restrictions on MHADA flats — specified period of no-resale.

5. MAHARASHTRA SHOPS AND ESTABLISHMENTS ACT, 1948
(a) Registration mandatory for all commercial establishments.
(b) Working hours: 9 hours per day, 48 hours per week.
(c) Overtime: Double wages.
(d) Leave: 21 days earned leave per year.

6. MUMBAI METROPOLITAN REGION REGULATIONS
(a) BMC (Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation): Property tax, building approvals, trade licenses.
(b) MCGM (Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai): Same as BMC.
(c) Development Control Regulations: FSI (Floor Space Index) rules, building height restrictions.
(d) CRZ (Coastal Regulation Zone): Restrictions on construction near coastline.

7. MAHARASHTRA PROFESSIONAL TAX
(a) Maximum Rs. 2,500 per year for employees.
(b) Deducted monthly from salary and deposited with state government.

8. MAHARASHTRA REAL ESTATE (REGULATION AND DEVELOPMENT) ACT — MahaRERA
(a) Website: maharerait.maharashtra.gov.in
(b) Strong enforcement track record — highest number of complaints resolved.
(c) All projects with 8+ units or 500 sq. m.+ must be registered.
(d) Online complaint filing available.
