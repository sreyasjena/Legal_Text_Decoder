RAJASTHAN STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
============================================
State-specific legal reference for Rajasthan laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN RAJASTHAN
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN RAJASTHAN
(a) Stamp duty rate: 5% for men, 4% for women of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 1% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Rajasthan — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Rajasthan through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-RA-... for Rajasthan e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN RAJASTHAN
(a) Registration portal: apnakhata.rajasthan.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN RAJASTHAN
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN RAJASTHAN
Applicable law: Rajasthan Rent Control Act 2001
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN RAJASTHAN
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN RAJASTHAN
RERA portal: rera.rajasthan.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 4: STATE-SPECIFIC LAWS AND REGULATIONS

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN RAJASTHAN
(a) Professional tax in Rajasthan: Not applicable.
(b) RIICO: Rajasthan industrial infrastructure corporation.
(c) JDA (Jaipur Development Authority): Development authority.
(d) Rajasthan Tenancy Act 1955: Agricultural tenancy.
(e) Rajasthan Shops and Commercial Establishments Act 1958.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR RAJASTHAN
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR RAJASTHAN
(a) Always verify title documents in Rajasthan revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Rajasthan RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Rajasthan property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Rajasthan.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Rajasthan Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Rajasthan police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Rajasthan.
