ASSAM STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
========================================
State-specific legal reference for Assam laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN ASSAM
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN ASSAM
(a) Stamp duty rate: 8.25% of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 1% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Assam — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Assam through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-AS-... for Assam e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN ASSAM
(a) Registration portal: revenueassam.nic.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN ASSAM
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN ASSAM
Applicable law: Assam Urban Areas Rent Control Act 1972
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN ASSAM
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN ASSAM
RERA portal: RERA Assam established under central framework
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

SECTION 4: STATE-SPECIFIC LAWS AND REGULATIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN ASSAM
(a) Professional tax in Assam: Up to Rs. 2,400 per year.
(b) GMDA (Guwahati Metropolitan Development Authority).
(c) Assam Shops and Establishments Act 1971.
(d) Inner Line Permit: Required for non-Assam residents in certain areas.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR ASSAM
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR ASSAM
(a) Always verify title documents in Assam revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Assam RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Assam property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Assam.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Assam Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Assam police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Assam.
