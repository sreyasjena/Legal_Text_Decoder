UTTARAKHAND STATE SPECIFIC LAWS KNOWLEDGE BASE
==============================================
State-specific legal reference for Uttarakhand laws, property regulations, and compliance requirements.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: STAMP DUTY AND REGISTRATION IN UTTARAKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

1. STAMP DUTY IN UTTARAKHAND
(a) Stamp duty rate: 5% for men, 3.75% for women of property value.
(b) Registration charges: 2% of property value.
(c) Stamp duty is calculated on the higher of: agreement value or circle rate/guidance value.
(d) Women buyers may get reduced stamp duty in Uttarakhand — verify current rates.
(e) e-Stamp: Available for Uttarakhand through SHCIL and authorized banks.
(f) Certificate number format: IN-UT-... for Uttarakhand e-stamps.

2. PROPERTY REGISTRATION IN UTTARAKHAND
(a) Registration portal: bhulekh.uk.gov.in
(b) All sale deeds for immovable property must be compulsorily registered.
(c) Documents required: Sale deed, ID proof of both parties, PAN cards, property documents, NOC if applicable.
(d) Biometric verification at Sub-Registrar office.
(e) Encumbrance Certificate: Available through registration portal — shows all registered transactions.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: RENT CONTROL IN UTTARAKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

3. RENT CONTROL ACT IN UTTARAKHAND
Applicable law: Uttarakhand Urban Premises (Rent Control) Act 1976
(a) Governs residential and commercial tenancies within the scope of the Act.
(b) Leave and license agreements properly executed are generally outside Rent Control Act protection.
(c) 11-month convention: Common practice to avoid Rent Control Act protection and mandatory registration.
(d) Disputes: Approach Rent Authority or Civil Court as applicable.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: REAL ESTATE REGULATION IN UTTARAKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

4. RERA IN UTTARAKHAND
RERA portal: ukrera.uk.gov.in
(a) All residential/commercial projects with area >500 sq. m. or 8+ units must be registered.
(b) Verify project registration before booking any under-construction property.
(c) File complaints online through the RERA portal for project delays or disputes.
(d) Homebuyer rights under RERA: Interest on delayed possession, refund with interest, compensation.

5. KEY STATE-SPECIFIC PROVISIONS IN UTTARAKHAND
(a) Non-residents cannot purchase agricultural land in Uttarakhand.
(b) Professional tax in Uttarakhand: Up to Rs. 2,400 per year.

SECTION 5: GENERAL LEGAL ADVICE FOR UTTARAKHAND
─────────────────────────────────────────

6. PROPERTY TRANSACTION ADVICE FOR UTTARAKHAND
(a) Always verify title documents in Uttarakhand revenue record format.
(b) Check state-specific stamp duty before property registration.
(c) Verify RERA registration of under-construction projects on Uttarakhand RERA portal.
(d) Consult a local advocate familiar with Uttarakhand property laws for complex transactions.
(e) Check for agricultural land conversion requirements specific to Uttarakhand.
(f) Verify Encumbrance Certificate from Uttarakhand Sub-Registrar office.
(g) Conduct police verification of tenants as required by Uttarakhand police department.
(h) Check society/association NOC requirements for apartment transactions in Uttarakhand.
